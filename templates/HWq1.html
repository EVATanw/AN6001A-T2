Cloud platforms offer significant advantages for real-world AI projects in two key aspects:

  • Flexible and Scalable Storage: They support diverse storage types, such as object storage, distributed file systems, and databases, enabling the storage of large datasets for AI projects. These storage solutions are highly scalable, accommodating the growing data requirements of real-world applications.
  • High-Performance Computing Resources: Cloud platforms provide virtual machines equipped with efficient GPUs, powerful CPUs, and ample memory, ensuring the computational power needed to process large datasets and train complex AI models effectively.
